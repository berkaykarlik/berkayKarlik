Hello visitor!
It's great to see you in my little blog. I'm Berkay. A full-stack developer by day
and a lazy geek at night. That's exactly why I have decided to start my own blog.

I always thought that you need to master your craft first to share your work.
However, after reading some articles and watching some videos I have realized that
contribution can be helpful at any level. If I had learned just one topic
in a completely new field, I would be one topic more experienced than the person just started.

Another good reason for me to start this blog is adding more self expression to what I do.
Programming in a vacuum is quite solitary. Without feedback and critism it's hard to improve.
Furthermore, you need to be able to explain the projects and the products to other people effectively.
Otherwise, they won't get used even if they were state of the art.

Thus, I plan to blog regularly, at least once a week. Main topic will be technology but posts can be
about anything I learned recently. Hope any reader, that would join the ride along they way, can find something of value!
